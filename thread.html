THREAD

Recycling App with UX Research

View Presentation

Summary

Thread is a recycling app designed to improve waste classification and user engagement through UX/UI design. Based on user interviews, AS-IS analysis, and usability research, Thread integrates practical and intuitive recycling education into daily life.

Develop Period

  • March 2024 – June 2024

Platform

  • Figma: UI/UX design and user flow prototyping
  • Google Slides: Presentation & research report

Team

  • Team Size: 4 Members
  • Lee Jeonghyeon: UX Research & Design Planning
  • Other members: Interviews, Persona Analysis, UI Mockups

Main Features

  • User education for proper recycling with gamified missions
  • Daily recycling checklist to encourage habit formation
  • Product barcode scanning for disposal instructions
  • Badge & level system for user motivation

UX Process

  • Interviewed 10+ users to gather needs and pain points
  • Conducted AS-IS analysis of existing recycling apps
  • Defined personas and created journey maps
  • Designed wireframes and interactive prototype with Figma
